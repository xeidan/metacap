FAQ

Frequenty asked questions

What services does your trading company offer?

We offer a range of services, including comprehensive trading education, trading signals for clients seeking trading opportunities, and account management services for both individual traders and prop firm accounts.

How can I benefit from your trading education?

Our trading education equips you with the knowledge and skills needed to become a successful trader. We provide in-depth courses, webinars, and resources to help you understand the markets, analyze data, and make informed trading decisions.

Can I get a refund for my subscription?

We offers a 30-day money-back guarantee for most of its subscription plans. If you are not satisfied with your subscription within the first 30 days, you can request a full refund. Refunds for subscriptions that have been active for longer than 30 days may be considered on a case-by-case basis.

What are trading signals, and how do they work?

Trading signals are alerts generated by our experienced analysts that indicate potential trading opportunities. Subscribers receive these signals via Telegram or WhatsApp, along with recommended entry and exit points, allowing them to follow the market's momentum.

Can I customize the trading signals to my preferences?

Yes, you have the flexibility to choose which trading signals to follow based on your trading strategy and risk tolerance. Our signals provide valuable insights, but the final decision to execute a trade is yours.

How does your account management service work?

Our account management service allows you to delegate the management of your trading account to our professional team. We trade on your behalf, following a strategy tailored to your goals and risk tolerance, aiming for consistent profitability.

Do you manage prop firm accounts as well?

Yes, we offer account management services for proprietary trading firms. Our team specializes in optimizing trading strategies to meet the specific goals and requirements of prop firms.

What sets your trading company apart from others?

We differentiate ourselves by combining top-tier education, accurate trading signals, and expert account management under one roof. Our holistic approach aims to empower traders with the knowledge and resources they need to succeed.

How can I get started with your services?

Getting started is easy. Simply sign up for our education courses, subscribe to our trading signals, or reach out to discuss account management options. Our team is here to assist you on your trading journey.

Is there a trial period for your services?

We offer trial periods for our education and trading signal services to give you a taste of what we offer. However, account management services are more tailored and typically require a consultation to get started.

What is your track record for profitability with account management services?

Our account management services aim to achieve consistent profitability, but past performance is not indicative of future results. We work diligently to manage risk and maximize returns, adhering to sound trading principles.
